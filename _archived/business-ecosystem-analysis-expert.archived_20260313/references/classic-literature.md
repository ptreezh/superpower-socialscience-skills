## 🎯 核心概念

### Business Ecosystem (商业生态系统)
> "An economic community supported by a foundation of interacting organizations and individuals."

### Keystone (基石物种)
> "Firms that provide the foundation upon which the ecosystem is built."

### Co-evolution (协同进化)
> "The process by which ecosystem members evolve together."

### Dynamic Capabilities (动态能力)
> "The firm's ability to integrate, build, and reconfigure competencies."
