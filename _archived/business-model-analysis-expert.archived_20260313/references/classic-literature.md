## 🎯 核心概念

### Business Model Canvas (9个构建模块)

1. **Customer Segments** - Who do we serve?
2. **Value Propositions** - What do we offer?
3. **Channels** - How do we reach them?
4. **Customer Relationships** - How do we interact?
5. **Revenue Streams** - How do we earn?
6. **Key Resources** - What do we need?
7. **Key Activities** - What do we do?
8. **Key Partnerships** - Who helps us?
9. **Cost Structure** - What do we spend?

### Value Creation
> "The mechanism by which value is created and captured from innovation."

### Business Model Innovation
> "Reinventing the way value is created and delivered."
