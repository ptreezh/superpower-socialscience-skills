# Complex Adaptive Systems 权威经典文献

## 📚 创始人经典专著

### John H. Holland (1929-2015) - CAS理论创始人

**Holland, J. H. (1995). *Hidden Order: How Adaptation Builds Complexity*.**

- **核心贡献**:
  - Complex adaptive systems (CAS)
  - Emergence (涌现)
  - Agents (主体)
  - Echo model

**Holland, J. H. (1998). *Emergence: From Chaos to Order*.**

- **核心贡献**:
  - Emergence theory
  - "Much coming from little"

### W. Brian Arthur - 收益递增理论

**Arthur, W. B. (1994). *Increasing Returns and Path Dependence*.**

- **核心贡献**:
  - Increasing returns
  - Path dependence
  - Lock-in

### Stuart Kauffman - 自组织理论

**Kauffman, S. A. (1993). *The Origins of Order*.**

- **核心贡献**:
  - Self-organization
  - Fitness landscapes
  - NK model

### Agent-Based Modeling Founders

**Epstein, J. M., & Axtell, R. (1996). *Growing Artificial Societies*.**

- Sugarscape model
- Bottom-up social science

## 🎯 核心概念

### CAS (复杂适应系统)
> "Systems that have a large numbers of components that interact and adapt."

### Emergence (涌现)
> "Complex patterns arise from simple rules... 'Much coming from little'."

### Path Dependence (路径依赖)
> "Current choices constrained by past decisions... small events can have large effects."

### Increasing Returns (收益递增)
> "Adoption increases attractiveness... leading to lock-in."
