# Durkheimian Theory 权威经典文献

## 📚 创始人经典专著

### Émile Durkheim (1858-1917) - 社会学创始人之一

**Durkheim, É. (1893). *The Division of Labor in Society*.**

- **核心贡献**:
  - Mechanical solidarity vs. Organic solidarity
  - Anomie
  - Evolution of social solidarity

**Durkheim, É. (1895). *The Rules of Sociological Method*.**

- **核心贡献**:
  - Social facts
  - Sociological method

**Durkheim, É. (1897). *Suicide*.**

- **核心贡献**:
  - Four types of suicide
  - Social integration
  - Social regulation

**Durkheim, É. (1912). *The Elementary Forms of Religious Life*.**

- **核心贡献**:
  - Collective conscience
  - Sacred vs. Profane

---

## 🎯 核心概念

### Social Facts (社会事实)
> "A social fact is every way of acting, thinking, and feeling external to the individual, and endowed with a power of coercion."

### Suicide Types
- Egoistic (利己型)
- Altruistic (利他型)
- Anomic (失范型)
- Fatalistic (宿命型)

### Anomie (失范)
> "Anomie is a state of normlessness... breakdown of social regulation."

### Solidarity
- Mechanical (机械团结)
- Organic (有机团结)
