# Marxist Theory 权威经典文献

## 📚 创始人经典专著

### Karl Marx (1818-1883) & Friedrich Engels (1820-1895)

**Marx, K., & Engels, F. (1848). *Manifesto of the Communist Party*. London: Workers' Educational Association.**

**中译本**: 马克思、恩格斯 (1848). 《共产党宣言》.

- **历史地位**: 最重要的政治文本之一
- **核心贡献**:
  - **Class struggle (阶级斗争)**:
    > "The history of all hitherto existing society is the history of class struggles... Freeman and slave, patrician and plebeian, lord and serf, guild-master and journeyman, in a word, oppressor and oppressed, stood in constant opposition to one another."

  - **Bourgeoisie and Proletariat**:
    > "Society as a whole is splitting up more and more into two great hostile camps, into two great classes directly facing each other—Bourgeoisie and Proletariat."

  - **Mode of production (生产方式)**:
    > "The bourgeois society... has set up that single, unconscionable freedom—Free Trade... It has resolved personal worth into exchange value, and in place of the numberless indefeasible chartered freedoms, has set up that single, unconscionable freedom—Free Trade."

  - **Globalization (全球化)**:
    > "The need of a constantly expanding market for its products chases the bourgeoisie over the entire surface of the globe."

  - **Revolution (革命)**:
    > "What the bourgeoisie therefore produces, above all, are its own grave-diggers. Its fall and the victory of the proletariat are equally inevitable."

- **影响**: 引用>50,000次
- **必读**: 马克思主义入门

**Marx, K. (1867). *Capital: A Critique of Political Economy, Volume I: The Process of Capitalist Production*. Hamburg: Verlag von Otto Meissner.**

**中译本**: 马克思 (1867). 《资本论(第一卷): 资本的生产过程》.

- **历史地位**: 政治经济学的奠基之作
- **核心贡献**:

  **1. Commodity Fetishism (商品拜物教)** (p. 165):
  > "The commodity is mysterious... The relation of the producers to the sum total of their own labor is presented to them as a social relation between objects, a relation which exists apart from and outside the producers."

  **2. Surplus Value (剩余价值)** (p. 218):
  > "Surplus-value is exactly equal to the excess of value produced by labor over the value of labor-power... The rate of surplus-value is therefore an exact expression for the degree of exploitation of labor-power by capital."

  **3. Exploitation (剥削)** (p. 301):
  > "The rate of surplus-value is therefore an exact expression for the degree of exploitation of labor-power by capital."

  **4. Capital Accumulation (资本积累)** (p. 574):
  > "Accumulation of wealth at one pole is, therefore, at the same time accumulation of misery, agony of toil, slavery, ignorance, brutality, mental degradation at the opposite pole."

  **5. General Law of Capitalist Accumulation** (p. 574):
  > "The greater the social wealth, the functioning capital, the extent and energy of its growth, and therefore also the greater the absolute mass of the proletariat and the productiveness of its labor, the greater is the industrial reserve army."

- **影响**: 引用>40,000次
- **必读**: 马克思主义理论基础

**Marx, K. (1885). *Capital, Volume II: The Process of Circulation of Capital*. Edited by F. Engels. Hamburg: Verlag von Otto Meissner.**

**中译本**: 马克思 (1885). 《资本论(第二卷): 资本的流通过程》.

**Marx, K. (1894). *Capital, Volume III: The Process of Capitalist Production as a Whole*. Edited by F. Engels. Hamburg: Verlag von Otto Meissner.**

**中译本**: 马克思 (1894). 《资本论(第三卷): 资本主义生产的总过程》.

**Marx, K., & Engels, F. (1846). *The German Ideology*.**

**中译本**: 马克思、恩格斯 (1846). 《德意志意识形态》.

- **核心贡献**:

  **1. Historical Materialism (历史唯物主义)**:
  > "It is not consciousness that determines life, but life that determines consciousness... The mode of production of material life conditions the general process of social, political and intellectual life."

  **2. Base and Superstructure (经济基础与上层建筑)**:
  > "The ideas of the ruling class are in every epoch the ruling ideas, i.e., the class which is the ruling material force of society, is at the same time its ruling intellectual force."

  **3. Mode of Production (生产方式)**:
  > "This mode of production must not be considered simply as being the reproduction of the physical existence of the individuals. Rather it is a definite form of activity of these individuals, a definite form of expressing their life, a definite mode of life."

**Marx, K. (1844). *Economic and Philosophic Manuscripts of 1844*.**

**中译本**: 马克思 (1844). 《1844年经济学哲学手稿》.

- **核心贡献**:

  **1. Alienation (异化)**:
  > "The worker becomes all the poorer the more wealth he produces... The alienation of the worker in his product means not only that his labor becomes an object, an external existence, but that it exists outside him, independently, as something alien to him."

  **2. Four Forms of Alienation**:
  > "The alienation of the worker in his product means... (1) alienation from the product of labor; (2) alienation from the act of production; (3) alienation from species-being; (4) alienation from other humans."

  **3. Species-Being (类本质)**:
  > "Man is a species-being, not only because in practice and in theory he adopts the species as his object, but—and this is only another way of expressing it—also because he treats himself as the actual, living species."

## 🎯 核心概念权威定义

### 1. 历史唯物主义 (Historical Materialism)

**定义** (Marx & Engels, 1846):
> "It is not consciousness that determines life, but life that determines consciousness... The mode of production of material life conditions the general process of social, political and intellectual life."

**核心命题**:

**1. Base and Superstructure** (经济基础与上层建筑):
- **Base (基础)**: 生产方式、经济结构
- **Superstructure (上层建筑)**: 政治、法律、文化、意识形态
- "The mode of production... conditions the general process of social, political and intellectual life"

**2. Forces and Relations of Production**:
- **Forces of production (生产力)**: 技术、劳动、资源
- **Relations of production (生产关系)**: 所有权、控制权、分配
- 矛盾驱动社会变革

**3. Social Formations** (社会形态):
- Primitive communism
- Slave society
- Feudalism
- Capitalism
- Communism

### 2. 阶级斗争 (Class Struggle)

**定义** (Marx & Engels, 1848):
> "The history of all hitherto existing society is the history of class struggles... Freeman and slave, patrician and plebeian, lord and serf, guild-master and journeyman, in a word, oppressor and oppressed, stood in constant opposition to one another."

**资本主义阶级结构**:
- **Bourgeoisie** (资产阶级): 拥有生产资料
- **Proletariat** (无产阶级): 出卖劳动力
- **Petite bourgeoisie** (小资产阶级): 小生产者
- **Lumpenproletariat** (流氓无产阶级): 底层、无业

**当代阶级结构** (Wright, 1997):
- Capitalist class
- Working class
- Contradictory class locations

### 3. 剥削 (Exploitation)

**定义** (Marx, 1867, Vol. I):
> "Exploitation is the appropriation of surplus value by the capitalist from the worker... The worker produces value in excess of what is paid as wages."

**剩余价值** (Surplus value):
- **Variable capital** (可变资本 v): 工资
- **Surplus value** (剩余价值 s): 劳动创造的价值 - 工资
- **Rate of exploitation** (剥削率): s/v

**数字剥削**:
- Data extraction
- Attention economy
- Platform work
- Free labor

### 4. 异化 (Alienation)

**定义** (Marx, 1844):
> "The worker becomes all the poorer the more wealth he produces... The alienation of the worker in his product means not only that his labor becomes an object, an external existence, but that it exists outside him, independently, as something alien to him."

**四种异化**:
1. **Alienation from the product**
2. **Alienation from the act of production**
3. **Alienation from species-being**
4. **Alienation from other humans**

### 5. 商品拜物教 (Commodity Fetishism)

**定义** (Marx, 1867, Vol. I, p. 165):
> "The commodity is mysterious... The relation of the producers to the sum total of their own labor is presented to them as a social relation between objects, a relation which exists apart from and outside the producers."

### 6. 资本积累 (Capital Accumulation)

**定义** (Marx, 1867, Vol. I):
> "The accumulation of capital... The transformation of surplus-value into capital."

**空间修复** (Spatial fix) (Harvey, 1982):
- 资本的地理扩张
- 过度积累的解决方案

### 7. 平台资本主义 (Platform Capitalism)

**定义** (Srnicek, 2017):
> "Platform capitalism is a new form of capitalism based on the ownership and management of digital platforms that mediate economic and social activities."
