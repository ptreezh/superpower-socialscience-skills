# Weberian Theory 权威经典文献

## 📚 创始人经典专著

### Max Weber (1864-1920) - 社会学创始人之一

**Weber, M. (1905). *The Protestant Ethic and the Spirit of Capitalism*.**

- **历史地位**: 最重要社会学著作之一
- **核心贡献**:
  - Protestant ethic
  - Spirit of capitalism
  - Calling (天职)
  - Asceticism (禁欲主义)
  - Rationalization (理性化)
  - "Iron cage" (铁笼)
- **影响**: 引用>50,000次

**Weber, M. (1922). *Economy and Society*.**

- **核心贡献**:
  - Social action (社会行动)
  - Types of authority (权威类型)
  - Bureaucracy (官僚制)
  - Ideal type (理想类型)
  - Verstehende Soziologie

**Gerth & Mills (Eds.) (1946). *From Max Weber*.**

- **核心论文**:
  - "Science as a Vocation"
  - "Politics as a Vocation"
  - "Bureaucracy"

## 🎯 核心概念

### Social Action (社会行动)
> "Social action is action that takes account of the behavior of others."

**Types**:
1. Instrumentally rational
2. Value-rational
3. Affectual
4. Traditional

### Types of Authority
- **Traditional**: 传统的神圣性
- **Charismatic**: 魅力型领袖
- **Legal-rational**: 法理型

### Bureaucracy (官僚制)
- Hierarchy
- Rules
- Impersonality
- Expertise

### Rationalization (理性化)
> "The process by which modes of thought and action become increasingly rational."

### Protestant Ethic (新教伦理)
> "Emphasized worldly calling, asceticism, and the accumulation of wealth as signs of salvation."
