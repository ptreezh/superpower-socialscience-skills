## 🎯 核心概念

### Stocks and Flows
- **Stocks**: Accumulations (bath tub)
- **Flows**: Rates (faucet, drain)

### Feedback Loops
- **Positive (R)**: Reinforcing
- **Negative (B)**: Balancing

### Causal Loop Diagrams
- Variable relationships
- Delay (D)
- Polarity (+ or -)
